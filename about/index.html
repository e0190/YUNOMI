The Philosophy

Architectural Dining

Crafting the Perfect Bite

Yunomi Handroll represents the union of minimal aesthetic style and meticulous Japanese technique. Founded in Los Angeles, our spaces are intentionally engineered around the central counter to cultivate an immediate connection between chef and diner.

Every handroll served is defined by structural balance: crisp, premium nori sourced overseas, precise warm seasoned rice, and perfectly chilled, responsibly caught fish. We believe that fine dining doesn't require a multi-hour commitment, just uncompromising focus.
