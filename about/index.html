The Architecture of Dining

Our Story

AIA Los Angeles Restaurant Design Award

Official Winner — Conceptual Space & Physical Execution

We believe the structural constraints of space dictate the performance of flavor. At Yunomi, our physical layout eliminates the distance between culinary execution and consumption. The handroll bar is configured as an continuous clean surface of raw concrete, timber accents, and strict lines.

This layout honors the exact properties of the handroll: seaweed loses its signature crisp snap within seconds of coming into contact with warm, seasoned rice. By structuring our rooms so that the chef serves directly across a hyper-minimalist threshold, we ensure absolute perfection with every transaction.
